Boris Johnson

Welcome to the website of Boris Johnson. Boris is the current Prime Minister of the United Kingdom and has been in office since July 2019. He is the leader of the Conservative Party and has been the Member of Parliament for Uxbridge and South Ruislip since 2015.

On this website, you can find out more about Boris, and his vision for the future of the UK. You can also find up-to-date news and announcements regarding his policy decisions and initiatives.
